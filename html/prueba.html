SPLOT

Years have passed since Kratos took his revenge against the Olympian Gods. Having survived the final fight against his father Zeus, Kratos now lives with his young son Atreus in the world of the Norse Gods, a hostile land inhabited by fierce monsters and warriors. Kratos will have to act as mentor and protector of his son, and he will have to master the rage and rage that he had for many years and tie up loose ends.
