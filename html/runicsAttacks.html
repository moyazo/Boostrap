    WEAPONS RUNIC ATTACKS(current)

Runic Attacks are special moves that must be added to the Leviathan Ax and Blades of Chaos in order to perform them in combat. There are two types: light and heavy, which are activated by holding L2 + R1 and L2 + R2, respectively.
