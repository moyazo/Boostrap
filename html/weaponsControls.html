CHAOS BLADE This ax is the mightiest weapon in all of the Norse lands, created by the two dwarf brothers, Sindri and Brok, to destroy Thor's very hammer and end his path of death and destruction. The Leviathan Ax was created for Faye, and she gave it to Kratos upon death.
LEVIATAN AXE The Blades of Chaos are Kratos' primary weapons in God of War, God of War: Chains of Olympus, and God of War: Ascension, and secondary weapons in God of War (2018) and God of War: Ragnarök. They are given to him by Ares, the god of war, when Kratos agrees to be his servant.
LANZA DE DRAUPNIR Among the different weapons that can be used in God of War: Ragnarok, we find the Draupnir spear. This new weapon, which you will get as part of the story, is fast and versatile, as well as being able to stick, duplicate and explode once it is impaled on an enemy or surface.
